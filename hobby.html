There is so much variety and excitement from watching many different formats of shows. Movies provide a quick story with lots of quick action, excitement, and not a lot of commitment. TV Shows are usually longer, can go deeper into the backstory and meaning of the show and its characters. The plot can be slower but have greater buildup. Animated shows can be more unrealiztic which gives them the oppurtunity to be more creative, explore new ideas, and have crazy action packed scenes.

The best thing about great shows is that there is so much more to them then just what you see in a casual watch.
